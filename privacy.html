Privacy policy

Privacy

Seismic Shifts is designed to be minimal in every direction, including in what it knows about you.

Microphone

The app uses the iPad's microphone to compute, in real time, a single number representing the current acoustic energy in the room. That number drives the trace you see on screen. The audio itself is processed in memory and never written to storage in any form. No recording of audio is created, retained, or recoverable.

What is stored on the device

The app may persist the visualisation data — one numerical value per second representing acoustic energy — to a plain CSV file on the device. These files exist solely on the iPad they are recorded on. They are excluded from iCloud backup. They are equivalent in character to a barometer record: a stream of numbers describing the shape of an environment over time, from which neither speech nor identity can be reconstructed.

Network

The app does not access the network. It contains no analytics, no remote logging, no telemetry, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party SDKs. It does not phone home in any sense.

Tracking

The app performs no tracking. It does not identify, profile, or follow users across applications or websites. Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework is not used because there is nothing to track.

Data sharing

No personal data is collected, so no personal data is shared with anyone.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. It collects no information about anyone, including children.

Permissions

The app requests one permission: microphone access. You may decline; the app will simply show no trace activity. You can revoke microphone access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Seismic.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds an optional feature that requires it — the change will be reflected here, and the app will request any new permissions explicitly.
