Support

Seismic Shifts is intentionally featureless — there are no settings, no menus, no in-app interactions. If something is wrong, it usually shows up as the app not opening or the trace not moving. A small set of things to check.

Common questions

The trace is flat. The app needs microphone permission. Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and confirm Seismic is enabled. If it is, the room may simply be very quiet — clap or speak near the iPad to verify a response.

The screen is black or the app froze. Quit the app (swipe up from the bottom and swipe Seismic off the top) and relaunch.

I am running it as an installation. Anything else I should know? The app is intended to run continuously. Plug the iPad into power, disable Auto-Lock under Display & Brightness, lock the orientation to landscape, and use Guided Access to prevent accidental exit. The app handles audio interruptions and recovers automatically.

What about my data? See the privacy policy. The short version: no audio is stored or transmitted, and the app has no network access.
