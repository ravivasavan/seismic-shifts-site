Support

Seismic Shifts is intentionally featureless — there are no settings, no menus, no in-work interactions. If something is wrong, it usually shows up as the work not opening or the trace not moving. A small set of things to check.

Common questions

The trace is flat. The work needs microphone permission. Open your device's privacy settings and confirm microphone access is enabled for Seismic. If it is, the room may simply be very quiet — clap or speak near the device to verify a response.

The screen is black or the work froze. Quit it and relaunch.

I am running it as an installation. Anything else I should know? Seismic Shifts is intended to run continuously. Plug the device into power, disable auto-lock, lock the orientation to landscape, and use a kiosk or guided mode to prevent accidental exit. The work handles audio interruptions and recovers automatically.

What about my data? See the privacy policy. The short version: no audio is stored or transmitted, and the work has no network access.
